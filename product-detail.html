Is this real A2 cow ghee?
Yes — Godaivyam is made entirely from milk of native-breed Indian cows, which produce A2 protein. We source from small partner dairies in Solapur, Maharashtra. A2 status is verified by independent FSSAI labs for every batch.
How long does an opened jar last?
A sealed jar lasts 9 months from manufacture. Once opened, store in a cool dry place, use a clean dry spoon, and it stays fresh 4-6 months easily. Ghee actually improves with age.
What's the best way to use this ghee?
High smoke point (~250°C) — perfect for tadka, sautéing, deep-frying, baking, or drizzling over hot dal-chawal. One teaspoon daily is the traditional Ayurvedic recommendation.
