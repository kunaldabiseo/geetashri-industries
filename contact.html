Before You Write

Maybe one of these helps?

The questions we get most often. If yours isn't here, message us — we'd love to help.

How quickly will you reply?
WhatsApp — usually within an hour during business hours (Mon-Sat 10am-7pm IST). Form submissions and emails — within 24 hours. We're a small team, so every reply is from a real person.
I need help with my order
Keep your order ID ready (format #GOD-XXXXXX) and WhatsApp us. We can edit address, change SKU, or cancel — usually within minutes.
Do you accept bulk / HoReCa orders?
Yes — restaurants, sweet shops, weddings, corporate gifting. Special pricing on 15L tins. Pick "Bulk / HoReCa" in the form above, or call Shri directly.
Can NRIs order Godaivyam?
Not directly from the website yet, but we work with trusted NRI gifting partners. WhatsApp us with your country and we'll arrange it.
How do I book a facility visit?
We love visitors! WhatsApp us at least 2 days in advance with your name, date and group size. Visits are Mon-Sat 10am-5pm by appointment.
